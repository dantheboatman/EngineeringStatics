You may feel like the answer to this problem is “too big”. Large answers are normal in problems like this because the moment of inertia involves raising lengths the fourth power.
If it really bothers you, you can convert the results from \(\text{mm}^4\) to \(\text{m}^4\text{,}\) but then the number will probably feel “too small” to you. It’s best not to worry about it.
