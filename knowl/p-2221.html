Finding \(I_y\) using vertical strips is relatively easy. Letting \(dA = y\ dx\) and substituting \(y = f(x) = x^3 +x\) we have
\begin{align*} I_y \amp = \int_A x^2\ dA\\ \amp = \int_0^1 x^2 y\ dx\\ \amp = \int_0^1 x^2 (x^3+x)\ dx\\ \amp = \int_0^1 (x^5 + x^3) dx\\ \amp = \left . \frac{x^6}{6} + \frac{x^4}{4} \right \vert_0^1\\ I_y \amp = \frac{5}{12}\text{.} \end{align*}
