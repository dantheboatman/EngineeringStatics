The centroid of the whole shape is found by applying (7.5.1)
\begin{align*} \bar{x} \amp = \frac{ \sum {\bar{x}_i}\ A_i}{\sum A_i} \amp \bar{y} \amp = \frac{ \sum {\bar{y}_i}\ A_i}{\sum A_i}\\ \bar{x} \amp = \frac{ (1.5)(18)+(3.5) (14)}{18 + 14} \amp \bar{y} \amp = \frac{ \sum (5)(18)+(1) (14)}{18 + 14}\\ \bar{x} \amp = \inch{2.375} \amp\bar{y} \amp = \inch{3.25} \end{align*}
