Substituting \(m_i\ g_i = W_i\) in (7.2.2) gives the equations for the center of mass.
\begin{equation} \bar{x}=\frac{\sum \bar{x}_{i} \ m_i\ g_i}{\sum \ m_i\ g_i} \quad \bar{y}=\frac{\sum \bar{y}_{i} \ m_i\ g_i}{\sum \ m_i\ g_i} \quad \bar{z}=\frac{\sum \bar{z}_{i} \ m_i\ g_i}{\sum \ m_i\ g_i}\text{.}\tag{7.3.1} \end{equation}
