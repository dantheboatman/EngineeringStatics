Item 3. Determine the moment about \(C\).
The moment about point \(C\) is found with the cross product (4.5.1) where the moment arm is the displacement vector from \(C\) to \(A\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \vec{r}_{CA} = \ft{(4 \ihat + 0 \jhat + 5 \khat )} \end{equation*}
\begin{align*} \vec{M}_C \amp = \vec{r}_{CA} \times \vec{F} \\ \amp = \begin{vmatrix} \ihat \amp \jhat \amp \khat \\ 4 \amp 0 \amp 5 \\ 2 \amp -4 \amp -1 \\ \end{vmatrix} \left(\frac{500}{\sqrt{21}}\right) \end{align*}
