Find final exam score \(FE\) so that your average grade \(\bar{G}\) is 70%.
\begin{align*} \bar{G} \amp= \frac{\sum G_i w_i}{\sum w_i}\\ 70 \amp = \frac{(40 \times 0.25) + (80 \times 0.25) + (90 \times 0.1) + (FE \times 0.4)}{(0.25 + 0.25 + 0.1 + 0.4 )}\\ FE \amp = \frac{70 (1) - (10 +20 + 9)}{0.4} = 77.5 \text{.} \end{align*}
