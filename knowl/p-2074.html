In a rotating shaft, power is equal to the torque times the angular velocity in radians per second.
\begin{equation} P = T \omega\text{,}\tag{9.5.1} \end{equation}
so
\begin{equation} P_\text{max} = M_\text{i} (\omega_\text{i}) = M_\text{o} (\omega_\text{o})\text{.}\tag{9.5.2} \end{equation}
