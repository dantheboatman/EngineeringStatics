Calculating the \(3 \times 3\) determinant can be reduced to calculating three \(2 \times 2\) determinants using the method of cofactors, as follows
\begin{equation} \vec{A} \times \vec{B} = +\begin{vmatrix} A_y \amp A_z \\ B_y \amp B_z \end{vmatrix} \ \ihat -\begin{vmatrix} A_x \amp A_z \\ B_x \amp B_z \end{vmatrix}\ \jhat +\begin{vmatrix} A_x \amp A_y \\ B_x \amp B_y \end{vmatrix}\ \khat\text{.}\tag{2.8.4} \end{equation}
