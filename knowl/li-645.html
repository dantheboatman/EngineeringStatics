Item 3. Find the Moment of Inertia.
The distance between the neutral axis is and the centroids of the subparts are
\begin{align*} d_\text{R} \amp = | \bar{y} - \bar{y}_\text{R} | = | 0.846 - (-0.5)| = \inch{1.346}\\ d_\text{L} \amp = | \bar{y} - \bar{y}_\text{L} | = | 0.846 - 1.98| = \inch{1.134}\text{.} \end{align*}
The moment of inertia of the rectangle about the \(x'\) axis
\begin{align*} (I_x')_\text{R} \amp = [\bar{I} + A d^2]_\text{R}\\ \amp = 0.667 + (8)(1.346)^2 \\ \amp = \inch{15.16}^4\text{.} \end{align*}
The moment of inertia of one angle about the \(x'\) axis
\begin{align*} (I_x')_\text{L} \amp = [\bar{I} + A d^2]_\text{L}\\ \amp = 17.3 + (4.75)(1.134)^2 \\ \amp = \inch{23.4}^4\text{.} \end{align*}
The moment of inertia of the built up beam about the neutral axis
\begin{align*} I_{x'} \amp = \sum (I_{x'})_i \\ \amp = 2 (I_{x'})_\text{L} + (I_{x'})_\text{R} \\ \amp = 2(\inch{23.4}^4) + \inch{15.16}^4\\ I_{x'} \amp = \inch{61.98}^4\text{.} \end{align*}
