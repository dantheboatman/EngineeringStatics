Item.

finding the scalar projection of one vector onto another vector using the dot product, (2.7.9)
\begin{equation*} \|\proj_{\vec{u}} \vec{M}\| = \hat{\vec{u}} \cdot \vec{M} \end{equation*}
This combined dot and cross product is a signed scalar value called the scalar triple product. A positive sign indicates that the moment vector points in the positive \(\hat{\vec{u}}\) direction.
