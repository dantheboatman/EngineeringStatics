Rigid Body Equilibrium.

A rigid body can rotate and translate so both force and moment equilibrium must be considered.
\begin{align*} \Sigma \vec{F} \amp = 0\\ \Sigma \vec{M} \amp = 0 \end{align*}
In two dimensions, these equations produce in two scalar force equations and one scalar moment equation. Up to three unknowns can be determined.
In three dimension, they produce three scalar force equations and scalar three moment equations. Up to six unknowns can be determined.
