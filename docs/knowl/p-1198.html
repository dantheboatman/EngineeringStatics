The interactive below shows a triangular truss supported by a pin at \(A\) and a roller at \(B\text{,}\) and loaded at joint \(C\text{.}\) You can see how the reactions and internal forces adjust as you vary the load at \(C\text{.}\) You can solve it by starting at joint \(C\) and solving for \(BC\) and \(CD\text{,}\) then moving to joint \(B\) and solving for \(AB\) Joint \(A\) can be used to check your work.
