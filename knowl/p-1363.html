By our assumption that \(g\) is constant on the surface of the earth, \(g_i\) can be factored out of the sums and drops out of the equation completely.
\begin{equation} \bar{x}=\frac{\sum \bar{x}_{i} m_i}{\sum m_i} \quad \bar{y}=\frac{\sum \bar{y}_{i} m_i}{\sum m_i} \quad \bar{z}=\frac{\sum \bar{z}_{i} m_i}{\sum m_i} \text{.}\tag{7.3.2} \end{equation}
