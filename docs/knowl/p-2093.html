The total moment exerted acting on the disc due to the friction forces is found by integrating the elements \(dM\) over the contact area. The moment of each element will be equal to the product of the coefficient of kinetic friction, the normal force pressure, the moment arm and the area of each element
\begin{equation*} dM=\mu_k p r\; dA\text{.} \end{equation*}
\begin{align*} M\amp =\int_A dM\\ \amp=\int_A \mu_k p r \; dA \end{align*}
