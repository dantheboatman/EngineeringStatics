Solution 3.2.7.a.1.

The entire weight of the wire and the spool is supported by the sling.

Remember that weight is not mass and mass is not a force. The total weight in is found by multiplying the total mass by the gravitational constant \(g\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} W \amp = m g\\ \amp = (m_w + m_s )\,g\\ \amp=(0.75 \textrm{ km} \cdot 927 \textrm{ kg}/\textrm{km} + 350 \textrm{ kg})\,g \\ \amp = 1045 \textrm{ kg} \cdot 9.81 \textrm{ m}/\textrm{s}^2 \\ \amp = 10300 \textrm{ N} \end{align*}
